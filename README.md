## 🛠 How to Use CineHub

### Search and Browse

You can enter movie titles into the search box or explore through categories like Action, Drama, Comedy, and more. The interface shows thumbnails and brief info for each movie.

### Streaming Movies

Once you pick a movie, click the play button. The movie begins streaming without ads or interruptions. Use the player controls to pause, skip, or adjust volume.

### Downloading Movies

To watch later offline:

- Click the download icon near the movie title.
- Choose where to save the file on your PC.
- Wait for the download to finish.

You can find downloaded movies in the ‘Downloads’ folder on your computer or directly inside the CineHub app under "My Downloads."

### Managing Downloads

You can pause, resume, or cancel any ongoing download from the download manager section in the app.

### Adjust Settings

CineHub has a simple settings panel where you can:

- Change video quality to higher or lower depending on your internet speed
- Set download folder location
- Enable or disable subtitles (if available)

## ⚙️ Troubleshooting

If you face any issues running CineHub, try these steps:

- Restart your computer and run CineHub again.
- Check your internet connection.
- Make sure your Windows version is up to date.
- Reinstall the app by downloading the latest installer again.
- Close other programs that use a lot of internet bandwidth.
- If a movie won’t play, try another one or restart the app.
